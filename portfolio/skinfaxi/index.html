Skinfaxi
Book

Skinfaxi is a yearly book published by the Reykjavík Junior College. The project was done in collaboration with Siggi Odds and Jónas Val.

Each of us made 2-3 display typefaces for the project and co-art-directed, designed and photographed interviews and fashion editorials.

Everything except for adverts is printed in duotone, metallic-gold and black
